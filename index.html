Alotofads — First Generation

This is the first generation of Alotofads. Alotofads aims to create a free, hassle-free webpage designed specifically for people to view ads. Alotofads seeks to solve a fundamental problem with internet webpage advertising. Advertising on these platforms is often predatory, designed to distract the viewer and get them to pay for something they otherwise wouldn't have bought.

Alotofads provides a platform for viewers to view ads on their own terms. Internet users can now view and click on ads at will.

Future additions of Alotofads will display ads from multiple ad networks such as Microsoft Ads, Amazon Ads, Adsterra Ads, Ezoic Ads, and EthicalAds. This will also allow users to seek out ads that they have seen on other webpages that they want to view again.

The current generation serves personalized ads that specifically target the viewer. Future generations will serve ads that are not personalized. Ads will be provided purely based on context.

Additionally, future generations of Alotofads will include a search feature that will contextualize the webpage. This will allow the ad viewer to seek out the exact type of ads they desire. Future iterations will also provide an adapted interface to organize and accommodate all types of ads and ad networks.

The current webpage serves only as a first generation of Alotofads. This generation shares none of the features that future generations will share. This generation does not have a contextualizing search feature. This generation does not have ads from multiple ad networks.

This generation has only a small subset of the dozens of ad types that this webpage will hold in the future. This generation has only a boxy black-and-white layout, where future generations will have a more intentional layout.

This generation has multiple bugs that will need to be resolved in the future. Come check back every few weeks — we are constantly working to improve our system.
